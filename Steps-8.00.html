How to create New measure. 8

Now we will create New measure that will help us to create better and flexible report.

*The firsts new measure is about the average energy created in each year(2013-2017)

 DAX formula for the average energy is

*.AvgGrd_201N = AVERAGE(global_power_plant_database[generation_gwh_201N])

*The second new measure is about the total day of operation thru out of the year.

DAX formula for operation day of the year is

*.Operation201N_Day =

((DIVIDE(

    SUM('global_power_plant_database'[generation_gwh_201N]),

    SUM('global_power_plant_database'[Power_(MW)]),0

)/24)*1000)

 

N.B N=3,4,5,6,7

Energy = Power * Time 

Units

W=Watt  H=hour   

Energy= WH

Power = W

Time =H
